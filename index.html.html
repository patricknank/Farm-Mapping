Specialist mapping service

Small area clearing & paddock tree mapping

Accurate QGIS mapping and written assessment for native vegetation clearing — from a single paddock tree to multi-hectare small area removals under NSW legislation.

Every tree is GPS-located, identified, assessed, and mapped. Every clearing area is spatially defined and documented to support compliance with the Biodiversity Conservation Act, Local Land Services Act, and applicable local planning instruments.

Paddock tree survey · QGIS

Paddock tree mapping

Every paddock tree GPS-located and assessed individually. Species, DBH, height, crown, condition, and retention value recorded in the field and mapped in QGIS. Outputs suitable for clearing applications, property management plans, and offset calculations.

  • Individual tree GPS coordinates recorded on-site
  • Species identification and biometric assessment (DBH, height, crown)
  • Structural condition and useful life expectancy (ULE)
  • Retain / clear recommendation per tree
  • QGIS map with tree point layer, species labels, and TPZ zones
  • Written paddock tree assessment report
  • Biodiversity offset calculation if required

Small area clearing assessment

Spatial assessment and documentation for small area native vegetation clearing proposals under NSW legislation. The clearing extent is accurately mapped in QGIS, vegetation communities are identified, and a written assessment is prepared for planning submission.

  • QGIS mapping of proposed clearing boundary and extent (m²/ha)
  • Vegetation community identification and mapping
  • All trees within clearing zone individually assessed
  • Heritage database check (AHIMS) for clearing area
  • Biodiversity Conservation Act and LLS Act compliance check
  • Written small area clearing assessment report
  • Pre- and post-clearing spatial records provided

On-ground clearing partner

I work alongside an experienced clearing contractor with direct NSW Government project history in small area native vegetation clearing and paddock tree removal. Once the QGIS mapping and assessment is complete, the clearing can proceed under the same coordinated engagement.

  • NSW Government project experience in small area clearing
  • Paddock tree removal to stump or full extraction
  • Works carried out to the approved clearing extent only
  • Complies with mapped TPZ and retain/remove recommendations
  • Post-clearing site photos and compliance record provided
  • Available across NSW for small to medium area projects
NSW Legislation

Small area and paddock tree clearing in NSW is regulated under the Biodiversity Conservation Act 2016, the Local Land Services Act 2013, and applicable Local Environmental Plans. The right mapping and documentation upfront avoids compliance issues and delays. All assessments are prepared with reference to current legislative requirements and the NSW Biodiversity Offsets Scheme.
